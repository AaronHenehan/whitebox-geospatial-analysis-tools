Remove single-cell pits

This tool can be used to remove single-grid-cell pits, or topographic depressions. These are grid cells in digital elevation models (DEMs) that have no neighbouring cells with a lower or equal elevation. The algorithm will raise the elevation of pit cells to the level of the lowest neighbour plus a small elevation value (0.0001). The user must specify the names of the input DEM and the output image. Larger-sized depressions can be removed with either depression filling or depression breaching.
